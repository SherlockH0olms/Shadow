## ðŸ“º Video Editing Tips

### Transitions
- Use simple fades (0.5-1 second)
- Avoid flashy transitions
- Keep it professional

### Text Overlays
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Roboto)
- Size: Large enough to read on mobile
- Color: White with dark shadow/outline
- Duration: 2-4 seconds per text

### Pacing
- Don't rush through sections
- Allow 1-2 seconds after important points
- Match visuals with narration
